How To Play:

There is an image generated by AI.

Your objective is to guess what sentence was used to generate it.

The sentence describes the image.

It only checks words that are fully filled in the boxes.

If the text boxes are green, you got the correct letter in the correct slot.

If it is red, that letter is not in that slot, and you have to put it in the EXACT box the letter is in.

Guess the Sentence!
